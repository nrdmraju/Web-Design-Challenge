City Latitude vs Wind Speed

This graph's purpose is to show the change in the wind speed based off of the latitude.
When following the data points on the graph we can see that at around -58 degrees latitude
is where wind speeds start appearing. As the latitude increases we can see that the concentration
of the data points showing wind speed increases drastically around 20 degrees latitude until 75 degrees latitude.
As we can see based on that trend, as the latitude increases there is a stronger presence of wind speeds. When the latitude reaches higher degrees
there is a presence of higher wind speeds being recorded.
